    Dear M. Murphy,

    I was looking for tool which will help me to improve the performance of my rugby team in Canada when I’ve discovered Hudl and your products. Then I realize suddenly that I discovered an innovative company, which look to create game changing products in my favorite fiels: Sports. All customers or users on facebook highlight the power of disruption and the good user-experience of your product, as well as  its efficiency for improving the life of athletes. 

    With an enterprising and volontary nature, I participated in the creation of an IT startup which provided a B to B platform related to big data analysis. I was the product manager of this startup when we were selected by international well-known tech companies (Yahoo, Salesforce, Google, Sprint, Linkedin ...) as one of the 15 most innovative startups of 2012. My very broad functions allowed me to acquire many skills in product management, such as:

        - Broadcasting the development roadmap with developer team

        - Insuring iterations with our users for the new features of the platforms

        - Defining marketing strategy, social media strategy and acquisition funnel

        - Creating and animating our users communities

    These 3 years as the Product Manager in a startup and my current experience as UX designer in a big company (Wolters Kluwer) taught me how to be very innovative in an agile and chaotic environment as well as it taught me how to implement and execute structure and processes that are critical for achieving scalable and navigating big company political waters. 

    These past experiences helped me to define my vision of a great Product Designer. It is for me a capital job because it is managing the team’s most important resource: TIME. A great product manager needs to know how to innovate through minimalism, carve down the scope of the product until it makes sense to the users. He must also build respect without formal authority from engineers and engineering managers to influence the development calendar by staying focus on the product and users. He needs to be able to remember that it is not because you can that you should. The Product Designer also has great communication skills allowing him to say ‘‘No’’ to most of users feature requests while keeping them happy and excited about the products. 

    After leaving France for Quebec a year ago, I’m now looking for a new challenges and I realize how lucky I would be if I could integrate such a dynamic and disruptive team like you have.I’ve always been looking to learn more, this past year, I’ve learned to code using HTML/CSS, jQuery and JavaScript in order to better express my ideas to the team I’m working with and I have the strong feeling that I could continue to learn a lot working for Hudl. I’ve also been once in San Francisco after being elected as the French most innovative startup of 2012 and I’m dreaming of being able to go back United States; people were so aware and opened-minded that it became a life’s objective. 

    I’m looking forward to have the opportunity to present you my past works and all my motivations to join your team.
 

Pierre Lafon
